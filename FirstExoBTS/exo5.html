Introduction to Monster Hunter


Monster Hunter is a game produced by Capcom , first launched on the Playstation 2 this hack and slash type of game was extremly popular. But what is Monster Hunter? In this game the player is a Hunter in a fantasy world and as such he needs to complete quests such as collecting items , capturing monster or even hunting big or small monster.
